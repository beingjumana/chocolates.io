Milk Chocolates

Milk chocolate is solid chocolate made with milk added in the form of milk powder, liquid milk, or condensed milk. Best milk chocolates

Cadbury chocolate is the brand leader in the United Kingdom.First produced by George Cadbury Junior in 1905, Cadbury Dairy Milk was made with a higher proportion of milk than previous chocolate bars, and it became the company's best selling product by 1914. It is the best selling milk chocolate bar in the UK, followed by Galaxy.
"Hershey process" milk chocolate is the most popular in the US. The process was invented by Milton S. Hershey, founder of The Hershey Company. The process uses fresh milk from local farms.
